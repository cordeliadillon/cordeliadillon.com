Hey there! I'm Cordelia!

I enjoy collecting hats shaped like food, playing Boggle, and circumambulating cities.

I'm a cartoonist.

I hold an MFA in Comics from California College of the Arts. I mostly draw autobio webcomics and am now working on my first book, a comic memoir about anxiety. I'm super into comics that are accessible to readers with vision impairments.

I'm a techie.

I work professionally as a web accessibility engineer, most recently at Dropbox and Salesforce. I'm passionate about making sure technology is a great experience for everyone. I love helping senior citizens improve their computer skills and doing tech talks on accessibility and universal design.
